# Note Walker Character Sheet — Script Outline

## Logline
A once-forgotten footnote messenger traverses the paper tower, revealing his dual-life memories and subtle powers as footnote glyphs ignite around him.

## Beat Breakdown
1. **Paper Tower Corridor** — Jian Zhuxing walks the stacked manuscript corridor with footnote glow trailing.
2. **Identity Close-ups** — Highlight his ink scar, ※7 tag, paper lantern, and note-covered bandages.
3. **Footnote Jump** — He steps onto glowing annotation marks and blinks to an upper balcony.
4. **Draft Overlap Vision** — Two timelines overlay around him as he examines ghosted scenes.
5. **Forbidden Dossier** — Jian opens an unsigned dossier naming him as a future protagonist.
6. **Resolve** — He tucks the script away, gaze set toward the unseen annotator as pages swirl.
