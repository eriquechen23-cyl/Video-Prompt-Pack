# Loop Terminal City — Rei Li Character PV Script

## Logline
Rei Li, the loop-weary underground DJ of Terminal City, teases the coming uprising as she relives fractured memories across a fifteen-second loop.

## Beat Breakdown
1. **Skyline Freeze** — Terminal City's twilight skyline with a paused blast cloud, timecode 23:00.
2. **Glitch Street** — Pedestrians stutter through a frame skip beneath erratic billboards.
3. **Radio Booth Intro** — Rei leans on the mic, greeting the city.
4. **Countdown Mark** — Close-ups of her 00:00 brand and clockwork irises pulsing.
5. **Last-hour Request** — She jokes about end-of-world song requests.
6. **Backwards Second** — Street scene rewinds one second with LOOP HUD.
7. **Echo Recorder** — Wrist recorder plays a distorted message to climb thirteen steps.
8. **Focus Drift** — Rei snaps from dissociation with a self-aware quip.
9. **Tower Flashback** — Monochrome memory atop the Terminal Tower facing the black sea.
10. **Rooftop Vow** — Present Rei stares at the tower from a neon rooftop.
11. **Network Awake** — Other remnants respond with synced HUD pings.
12. **Title Strike** — Tower surge, slogan reveal, 00:00 flare to black.

- VO #3 (JP): 「……またリセットされたなら、十三段目を数えて。」（歪んだ残響）／字幕：『……如果又被重置，就去數第十三級台階。』

- VO #5 (JP): 「今周は、一緒に最後まで行こう。」／字幕：『這一輪，我們一起走到最後，好嗎？』

## Visual Motifs
- Clock tick light ring in Rei's eyes and 00:00 collarbone brand.
- Hourglass earring, wrist recorder bandage, glitching neon signage.
- HUD overlays showing LOOP numbers and ECHO ONLINE indicators.

## Audio Motifs
- Lo-fi broadcast static, ticking layered over heartbeats, reverse-play glitches.
- Low synth pads with rising filtered noise before cuts.
- Distorted female whisper from recorder with tape warble.
